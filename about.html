About Us

Welcome to Nsiga Foundation

Nsiga Foundation(NF) is a non-Governmental oragnization aimed at transforming lives of vulenrable communities in Western Uganda. NFs' creation was inspired by Emmanuel Nsiga's personal experiences with cancer's devastating effects on his family, driving a committment to support those facing similar adversities.

Founded on the personal tragedy and resilient spirit of Emmanuel Nsiga, NF seeks to address the profound challenges of education, womens' empowerment, and health awareness amidst the backdrop of poverty, cultural barriers and limited access to essential services.

Vision

To eradicate poverty and improve the well-being of vulnerable groups in Uganda

Mission

To educate, support, and comfort the vulnerable, fostering a society where everyone can achieve their full potential.






Serving the underprevilaged and marginalized women, orphans, youth, the sick among others
